HAMBURGER OF THE WEEK

Time

Level: Easy Prep: 10 min Yield: 4 Hamburgers
Total: 25 min Cook: 15 min

BENGAL BURGER

26 reviews

"No more dry, lackluster, disapointing, senile burgers. These are juicy, and spices can be easily added or changes to suit anyones taste. Baste frequently with your favorite barbeque sauce. If you find the meat mixture to mushy, just add more bread crumps until it forms patties that hold their shape"

  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Spice Mix, optional, recipe follows
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 4 slices cheese, such as Cheddar, American cheese
  • 4 soft hamburger-style buns, split
  • 4 slices beefsteak, tomatoes, optional
  • Assorted lettuces and greens, such as iceberg
  • Assorted mustards, optional
  • Mayonnaise, optional
  • Ketchup, optional
